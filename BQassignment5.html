❓ Frequently Asked Questions

How do I adopt a pet? +
Simply browse our pets, submit an adoption application, and our team will guide you through the process.
What are the adoption fees? +
Fees vary from $50 to $250 depending on age and medical history, covering vaccinations and microchip.
Can I meet the pet before adoption? +
Absolutely! We encourage meet-and-greet sessions to ensure a perfect match for your family.
Do you have senior pets? +
Yes, we love senior companions! Many of them are waiting for a cozy retirement home.
